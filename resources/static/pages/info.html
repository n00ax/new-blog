About Me

"Fervently Exploring Technology and It's Humanistic Origins"

I have always been fascinated with things based on systems and schemes of design or
interaction. Although I would consider myself well invested in a multitude of fields ranging from historical
research to space exploration and aerospace, I would foremost regard computational and electronic
fields as my most invested thoroughfare. I began my fascination with technology around the age of six
when I discovered Python and wrote small scripts to do various automation tasks. From that point on
I expanded my interests gaining skills and knowledge ranging from systems programming to web development
During my years of work, I have become a technical evangelist for various methodologies, and continue
to expand my skills and gain new insights.
